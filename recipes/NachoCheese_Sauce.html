Nacho Cheese Sauce

A Nacho cheese sauce used to dip your chips which make it delicious

Description

Cheese sauce is made with cheese or processed cheese . Pour it over tortilla chips and finish with your choice of toppings ( jalapenos, and diced tomatoes are all crowd-pleasing options).

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 7 slices processed American cheese
  • Â½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Step1:Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step2:Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour until a paste forms.
  3. Step3:Pour in milk and stir until mixture thickens.
  4. Step4:Add cheese and salt; cook and stir until cheese has melted, about 15 minutes.
  5. Step5:It's ready Enjoy!
